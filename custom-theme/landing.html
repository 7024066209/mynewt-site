Apache Mynewt consists of four primary collaborative goals:

Utilities such as boot loaders, file systems, time-series data, and more

High-performance, highly-configurable networking stacks, starting with Bluetooth Low Energy 4.2

Simple, secure image and configuration management

Modularity and easy composability to optimally address application and hardware requirements
